Methodology & Assumptions

  1. Volumes are taken directly from the Adidas-provided estimates (July 2026).
  2. CIBT A1 rate (€35) is applied to all 997 A1/CoC cases regardless of Article type, since CIBT charges a flat A1 fee.
  3. CIBT PWN rate (€65) uses the MSW product fee from the existing CIBT rate card.
  4. Cozm CIBT share is calculated at 40% of the EUR fee (60/40 partner split).
  5. Article 12/13 split is not available in the volume data. Default assumes 100% Article 12 (€25), giving the most conservative standard-pricing figure.
  6. Standard pricing assumes Cozm services the client directly and retains 100% of the fee.
  7. All figures are annual and in EUR unless stated otherwise.
