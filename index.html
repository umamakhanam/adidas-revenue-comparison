Methodology & Assumptions

All calculations are clearly labelled for verification

  1. Volumes taken directly from the Adidas-provided estimates (July 2026).
  2. CIBT A1 rate (€35) applied to all 997 A1/CoC cases — CIBT charges a flat fee regardless of Article 12/13.
  3. CIBT PWN rate (€65) uses the MSW product fee from the existing CIBT rate card.
  4. Cozm CIBT share calculated at 40% of the EUR fee (60/40 partner split).
  5. Article 12/13 split not available in volume data. Default: 100% Article 12 (€25) for the most conservative standard-pricing figure.
  6. Standard pricing assumes Cozm services the client directly and retains 100%.
  7. All figures are annual (p.a.) and in EUR unless stated otherwise.
