Methodology & Assumptions

All calculations are clearly labelled for verification

  1. Volumes taken directly from the adidas-provided estimates (July 2026).
  2. CIBT A1 rate (€35) applied to both Article 12 and Article 13 cases (997 total A1/CoC). CIBT charges the same flat fee for both article types.
  3. PWN — CIBT does not provide PWN services for adidas. Cozm offers PWN directly at €50 per case (1,727 cases).
  4. Cozm CIBT share calculated at 40% of the EUR fee (60/40 partner split). Applies to A1/CoC only.
  5. Article 12/13 split defaults to 50/50 for modelling. Real adidas data (past 2 years): 766 single-destination (Art 12) / 679 multi-destination (Art 13) = 53%/47%. Adjustable via the slider.
  6. Standard pricing — Article 12: €25, Article 13: €35, PWN: €50. Cozm services the client directly and retains 100%.
  7. All figures are annual (p.a.) and in EUR unless stated otherwise.
